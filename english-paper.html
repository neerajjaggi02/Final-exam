PRACTICE EXAMINATION

Grade III English (2025-26)

Time: 1 Hour Total Marks: 30

SECTION A: LITERATURE

Q1. Give the meanings:

1. Dismal: Ans: Gloomy / Sad

2. Gingham: Ans: Checkered cotton fabric

SECTION B: LANGUAGE

Q2. Tenses (Past/Present/Future):

1. Yesterday, Dorothy _______ (walk) to the woods. Ans: walked

2. The Wizard _______ (live) in Oz now. Ans: lives

Q3. Adverbs (Identify the Kind):

1. The women walked slowly. Ans: Manner

2. I will go tomorrow. Ans: Time

Q4. Prepositions:

1. The dog sat _______ the table. Ans: under / on / near

2. Toto jumped _______ the basket. Ans: into / in

Q5. Conjunctions (and, but, because, or):

1. He was tired _______ he kept walking. Ans: but

2. She cried _______ she lost her way. Ans: because

Note: Descriptive answers (sentences) should be checked by a parent!
