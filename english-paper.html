SECTION A: LITERATURE (The Wizard of Oz & Three Wise Old Women)
Q1. Fill in the blanks from the text:

1. Dorothy lived in the midst of the great Kansas ________. Ans: prairies

2. The Scarecrow was stuffed with ________. Ans: straw

3. The Three Wise Old Women went for a walk on a ________ day. Ans: winter's

Q2. Short Answer Review (Type your answer):

Why did the Tin Woodman want a heart?
Ans: So he could love and be happy.

Where did the three women get stuck in the poem?
Ans: In a tall tree / on a ladder.

SECTION B: LANGUAGE (Grammar)
Q3. Subject and Predicate: Identify the underlined part:

1. The little girl laughed. Ans: Subject

2. The Wizard gave them gifts. Ans: Predicate

3. Toto barked loudly. Ans: Subject

Q4. Verbs and Tenses:

1. She _______ (see) a cyclone yesterday. Ans: saw

2. They _______ (be) happy today. Ans: are

3. I _______ (visit) my aunt tomorrow. Ans: will visit

Q5. Adverbs (Identify kind: Manner/Time/Place):

1. The lion roared fiercely. Ans: Manner

2. Put the basket there. Ans: Place

3. We should leave early. Ans: Time

Q6. Prepositions: [cite: 31]

1. The letter was sent _______ John. Ans: to [cite: 32, 34]

2. There are stars _______ the sky. Ans: in [cite: 33, 35]

3. The monkey climbed _______ the tree. Ans: up

Q7. Conjunctions (Join the sentences): [cite: 127]

1. I took a nap. I was tired.
Ans: I took a nap because I was tired. [cite: 128]

2. The phone was expensive. I did not buy it.
Ans: The phone was expensive, so I did not buy it. [cite: 79, 80]
